About Us

Bridging the gap between global medical manufacturers and local healthcare providers through trust and precision.

Based in Birtamode, Jhapa, Shree Chemihub Traders serves as a key intermediary between international medical manufacturers and local healthcare providers all over Nepal. We specialize in authorized distribution, technical bidding, and comprehensive institutional supply for hospitals, clinics, and pathology labs.

Our Mission

To provide a complete and seamless diagnostic solution by distributing high-tech, reliable laboratory machinery and surgical supplies to healthcare facilities across the nation.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and advanced medical equipment partner in Nepal, empowering healthcare professionals with cutting-edge technology to elevate patient care and diagnostic accuracy.
